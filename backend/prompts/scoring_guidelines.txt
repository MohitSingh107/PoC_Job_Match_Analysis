# Job Relevance Score (0-100)
Calculate based on weighted factors:

1. Relevant Skills Match (30%)
- Presence and depth of core Data Analytics skills from the pre-analyzed market data (most_demanded_skills)
- Quality over quantity - substantial skills, not just keywords
- Deduct if irrelevant skills dominate

2. Experience Relevance (20%) (Only if Intermediate/Experienced)
- Alignment with data analytics functions
- Quantifiable achievements with metrics
- For freshers: Weight redistributes to Projects (15%) and Skills (5%)

3. Domain Knowledge (20%)
- Experienced/Intermediate: Industry-specific analytics experience, business metrics understanding
- Freshers: Domain understanding through coursework, case studies, or targeted projects
- Internships count for all levels

4. Education (15%)
- Relevant degree/major in quantitative or technical fields
- Freshers: Include GPA/honors and relevant coursework
- Experienced: Lower importance if 3+ years experience

5. Projects & Certifications (15%)
- End-to-end analytics projects with clear outcomes
- Relevant certifications
- Freshers: Projects are critical
- Experienced: Projects should demonstrate complexity and impact

Deductions: -5 to -10 points for lack of quantification or unfocused content

### ATS Score (0-100)
Calculate based on:

1. Format & Parseability (30%)
- Simple single-column layout, no tables/graphics/images
- Standard fonts (10-12pt)
- Text-based PDF or .docx format
- No headers/footers with critical info
- Major deduction (-20 to -30) for complex layouts

2. Section Headers & Structure (25%)
- Standard section names: Experience, Education, Skills, Projects, Certifications
- Reverse chronological order
- Consistent date format (MM/YYYY)
- Clear job/project details: Title, Organization, Location, Dates

3. Keyword Optimization (25%)
- Natural inclusion of relevant keywords from skills list
- Keywords in context within experience/projects
- Action verbs throughout
- Acronyms spelled out on first use
- No keyword stuffing

4. Contact & Completeness (20%)
- Complete contact info at top: Name, phone, email, LinkedIn, location
- No errors or unexplained gaps (experienced/intermediate only)
- Appropriate length: 1 page (freshers), 1-2 pages (experienced)

Major Deductions: Tables (-15 to -20), Graphics (-20 to -25), Multi-column (-10 to -15), Missing contact (-15)
